## Quick Reference

### Volume Formulas
- Kubus: V = P × L × T
- Silinder: V = π × r² × h
- Bola: V = (4/3) × π × r³

### Concrete Mix (per 1m³)

### Docker Commands
- `docker ps -a` - List all containers
- `docker logs <name>` - View logs
- `docker restart <name>` - Restart container

### Systemd Commands
- `sudo systemctl status <service>`
- `sudo systemctl restart <service>`
